Tim Drake: The third Robin!

© DC Comics. Drawn by Serg Acuna

Character Profile

The third Robin and the only one who discovered Batman's real identity!

Rather than being recruited for the role, Tim became Robin of his own accord.

Though for a good part of Tim's tenure as Robin he had both parents, after the events of Identity Crisis,he would become an orphan and soon the adoptive son of Bruce Wayne!

Tim would then lead the Teen Titans and Young Justice, and also become Red Robin for a short time before returning to Robin.
