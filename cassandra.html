Cassandra Cain: Orphan to Batgirl!

Character Profile.

Cassandra Cain is the child of assassin David Cain and the world's deadliest fighter, Lady Shiva.

As a child she was trained as a fighter by Cain under the name Orphan, and was told not to ever speak which resulted in her becoming completely mute.

Cassandra eventually escaped her father and joined Batman and his ranks as the Batgirl!
