Stephanie Brown: Spoiler and Batgirl!

© DC Comics. Dustin Ngyuen.

Character Profile

Stephanie become Spoiler to thwart her father's criminals activities as the Cluemaster. Eventually she become one of the batgirls, and also for a small period she became robin!

For a while, Stephanie and Tim Drake dated, so she also appears quite a bit in Tim Drakes' Robin run.
