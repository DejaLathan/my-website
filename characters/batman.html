Bruce Wayne: Billionaire Playboy and The Bat!

Character Profile

Orphaned as a young boy after a robery gone wrong, Bruce Wayne became the Batman to prevent any other child from experiencing the trauma he went through.

Now known as a philanthropist and playboy to the public, Bruce Wayne secretly spends his nights as the Batman to fight the endless war against crime.
