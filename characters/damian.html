Character Profile

Damian Wayne is the biological son of Bruce Wayne and Talia Al Ghul. Damian's conception was unknown to Batman and thus his mother and grandfather Ras Al Ghul raised the child to be the heir to the al ghul empire

Eventually Talia revealed their son to Batman and Damian spent some time with his father, eventually becoming the fourth Robin

However, given Damian's extremely harsh training as a killer, his relationship with his father and his adoptive brothers would be tumultuous for a while as he struggled with his upbringing as the heir to the Al Ghuls and now to becoming a hero
