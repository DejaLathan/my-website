Character Profile

Barbara Gordon is the first Batgirl in the modern age. Although she originally wanted to become a cop like her father but he was not supportive. Tired of everyone not taking her seriously she adopts the batgirl persona

While sitting at home with her father Jim, the Joker and his henchmen break in an attack the two. Leaving Barbara Gordon paralyzed and bound to a wheelchair. During this time she adopts the persona Oracle

For the past decade however, Barbara's ability to walk has since been restored and she currently works as both Oracle and Batgirl
