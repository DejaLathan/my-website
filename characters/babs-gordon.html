Barbara Gordon: Batgirl and Oracle

©DC Comics. Guillem March

Character Profile

Barbara Gordon is the first Batgirl of the modern age. Although she initially aspired to be a police officer like her father, he was unsupportive of her ambitions. Frustrated by being taken seriously, she adopted the Batgirl persona.

While at home with her father, the Joker and his henchmen broke in and attacked them, leaving Barbara paralyzed and confined to a wheelchair. As a result of this, she embraced the identity of Oracle.

As Oracle, Barbara played a crucial role in forming the team known as the Birds of Prey.

Initially, Oracle led the group, joined by Black Canary and the Huntress. Over time, it evolved into a rotating team of heroines.

In the past decade, Barbara's ability to walk has been restored, and she currently operates as both Oracle and Batgirl.
