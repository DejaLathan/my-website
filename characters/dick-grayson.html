Character Profile:

Dick Grayson grew up in a travelin circus and performed as an acrobat with his parents as The Flying Graysons. After witnessing their brutual death, Dick was taken in by Bruce Wayne.

After his time as Robin, Dick eventually left Gotham to form his own identity outside of being the Boy Wonder and became the leader of the Teen Titans - eventually adopting his own persona called Nightwing
