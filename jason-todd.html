Jason Todd

© DC Comics. Used for educational purposes. Batman and all related characters and stories are property of DC Comics

As a child Jason Todd was left to care for his drug-addicted step-mother as his father was sent to jail. One night, Jason attempted to steal the wheels off of a vehicle - the batmobile.

Eventually Jason would become the second robin, however soon he would discover the identity of his biological mother and in search of his mother he encountered the Joker.

Joker brutually beat the young robin and left him for dead. However, Jason would later be resurrected and return as the Red Hood.
