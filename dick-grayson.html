Dick Grayson: The First Boy Wonder!

Dick Grayson grew up in a traveling circus and performed as an acrobat with his parents as The Flying Graysons.

After witnessing their brutual death, Dick was taken in by Bruce Wayne.

Dick eventually left Gotham to form his own identity outside of being the Boy Wonder and became the leader of the Teen Titans.

He adopted his own persona, Nightwing, and found a city of his own to protect called Bludhaven.
